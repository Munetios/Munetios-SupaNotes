Are my notes private? expand_more
Yes. Notes are encrypted end-to-end using a key generated and stored only on your device. Nobody else — including Munetios — can read your notes.
Where are my notes stored? expand_more
Notes are stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB. They never leave your device unless you explicitly export them.
What happens if I clear my browser data? expand_more
Clearing browser data will permanently delete your notes and encryption key. Always export a backup from Settings before clearing site data.
Can I use SupaNotes offline? expand_more
As of now, It doesn't have service worker support, so offline functionality is limited. You can still use it in your browser while online.
How do I restore deleted notes? expand_more
Deleted notes move to the Trash page first. Open Trash and tap Restore to recover them before they are permanently deleted.
How do I reset my encryption key? expand_more
Go to Settings → Reset Encryption Key. Warning: this will make all existing notes unreadable. Export your notes as a backup first.
Is there a mobile app? expand_more
SupaNotes is a PWA and works great on mobile. Install it to your home screen from Chrome or Safari for a native-like experience with offline support.
How do I change the app language? expand_more
Open Settings and tap the Language selector to choose from over 10 supported languages.
